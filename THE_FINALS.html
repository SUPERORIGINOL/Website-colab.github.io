Introduction

Developed by Embark Studios, "THE FINALS" is an arena shooter set in the year 2100, 70 years into a dystopian future. In this world, society has become deeply immersed in virtual reality, escaping the harsh realities of the physical world, or what they call the "meatspace". The game draws inspiration from the novel "Ready Player One", presenting a future where the populace spends all their time in a virtual reality setting. Players compete in high-stakes, televised tournaments, battling for fame and glory in a world where the line between reality and not is increasingly blurred.

Gameplay

The basis of THE FINALS is its three classes, the Heavy, Medium and light. Each class has a proportional health and movement speed, with the Heavy having the most health but the is the slowest, with the Light having the least HP, while being the fastest. The medium falls in the middle of the two, playing more of a support role.

Gadgets

In "THE FINALS", players have access to a variety of gadgets that enhance their gameplay experience and provide strategic advantages. These gadgets range from deployable shields that offer temporary protection, to grappling hooks that allow for quick traversal across the map. Additionally, players can use explosive devices such as grenades and mines to control areas and eliminate opponents. Each class has access to unique gadgets that complement their playstyle, ensuring that players can tailor their loadouts to suit their preferred strategies. The effective use of gadgets can often be the difference between victory and defeat in the fast-paced, dynamic battles of "THE FINALS".

Maps

"THE FINALS" features a variety of maps that provide diverse environments for players to engage in intense battles. From sprawling urban landscapes to claustrophobic industrial complexes, each map offers unique challenges and opportunities for players to showcase their skills. Whether you prefer close-quarters combat or long-range engagements, there is a map that caters to your playstyle in "THE FINALS". Additionally, the dynamic nature of the maps ensures that no two matches are ever the same, keeping players on their toes and encouraging adaptability and creativity in their strategies.

Competitive Play

"THE FINALS" offers a competitive multiplayer experience that pits players against each other in fast-paced, high-stakes battles. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the arena shooter genre, there is a place for you in the competitive scene of "THE FINALS". With a variety of game modes and maps to choose from, players can test their skills and teamwork in a range of scenarios that challenge their reflexes, strategy, and communication. Whether you're looking to climb the ranks and prove yourself as the best player in the world, or simply enjoy some casual matches with friends, "THE FINALS" has something for everyone.
